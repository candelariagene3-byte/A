About the College of Education

The College of Education of the University of Rizal System – Morong Campus is dedicated to producing highly skilled and ethically-grounded teachers. We emphasize hands-on pedagogy, community-centered research, and professional development to ensure our graduates are ready for diverse classrooms.

Vision, Mission, Quality Policy & Core Values

Vision

The leading University in human resource development knowledge and technology generation and environmental stewardship.

Mission

The University of Rizal System is committed to nurture and produce upright and competent graduates and empower community through relevant and sustainable higher professional and technical instruction, research ,extension and production services.

Quality Policy

The University of Rizal System commits to deliver excellent products and services to ensure stakeholders' satisfaction in instruction, research, extension, production and dynamic administrative support and to continiously improve its Quality Management System processes to satisfy all applicable requirements.
